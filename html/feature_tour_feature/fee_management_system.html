Feature of School Fees Management System

Complete Fee Management

  • Fedena incorporates all types of fee structures followed by different institutions worldwide.
  • Schedule fee collection dates & do fees setup based on student category or course-wise in a few clicks.
  • Create Master Particular and Discounts in order to avoid complexity involved in dealing with multiple fee heads.
  • Using Fedena Mobile App, students or parents can pay fees from anywhere at any time.

Fee Receipt & Advance Fee Payment

  • Design fee receipt template & customize receipts headers & footers using the fee receipt templates.
  • View student, employee and guest fee receipts from a single dashboard
  • Invoice is available for all pre-scheduled fees like general fees, hostel fees, and transport fees.
  • Collect fees in advance and use it later for the future fee’s payment such as transport fees, academic fees and more.

Fee Defaulter & Online Payment

  • Check the fee defaulters report and remind students/parents about the pending fees via sms/email.
  • Fees can be collected online by integrating Fedena with options of Payment Gateways available.

Fee & Other Finance Reports

  • View income & expense transactions for all account under Transaction reports
  • Record the institute's everyday transactions by creating the custom categories.
  • Easily generate particular-wise student & daily transaction report, also payment mode summary report
  • Easily compare financial transactions of two different periods & generate tax collection report in one go

Instant Discount & Tax Calculation

  • Provide fixed/instant discounts along with late fees/fine collection.
  • Enables tax on all types of fee transactions – general fees, instant fees, transport, and hostel fees.
  • Privileged employees based on their requirement can do fees refund/revert.
  • Create & manage applicable tax slabs such as VAT, GST and more

Other Finance Transactions

  • Manage staff salaries and income tax deduction, also instantly generate salary slip of staff.
  • Effortlessly create multiple Donations and administer asset liability management
  • Specific controlled access can be provided to users to access Finance.
  • Group various transactions under fee account

Benefits of Fees Management System

No more standing in long queues parents can make fee remittances faster from website and mobile devices
Secured online payment & admin can easily monitor suspicious and fraudulent transactions.
Handle multiple finance operations for a group of institutions from a single platform.
