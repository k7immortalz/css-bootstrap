Feature of Gradebook and Examination Management System

Exam Management

  • Edit or delete exam schedule at any time
  • Connect Exam feature, fast forward the final grade/marks calculation of the necessary exam group.
  • In a tab, schedule exam for all batches of a class at a same time
  • Easily edit the mark of the students for active and inactive batches
  • Flexibility for teachers to enter marks and update it whenever necessary.

Generate Detailed Reports

  • Create batch & course wise exam report
  • Create a subject or assessment wise report for CCE, ICSE, CWA, GPA or CBSE grading system.
  • Customize report card field settings like Roll Number, Father’s name, DOB etc.
  • Easy, quick assessment and publishing of exam reports
  • Instantly send exam result alerts & notifications to parents

Gradebook Management

  • Import marks in bulk. Also, import planner and set up for a new academic year.
  • Gradebook provide student, subject and consolidated reports at one place.
  • Create student activity & marks based grading exams.
  • Skill-based mark entry for each subject can be shown in the report card.
  • With new set of Report Card Templates, customise the look of the term & planner reports
  • Show students records & remarks in Gradebook Reports against each subject in exam planner.

Online Examination

Move forward from paper-based examination and automate the whole examination process effortlessly. Institute can conduct various types of examinations online such as admission entrance test, employee recruitment test, surprise classroom test, subject or topic wise test and more. After evaluating the examination teachers can publish the result, and instantly a notification is sent to students/parents. Easily students can login in their account and view the result from anywhere at any time. Learn features in detail.

Benefits of Online Exam Management System

Instant result processing and quick feedback to students
Save time & eliminate the unnecessary cost of paper usage
Cloud storage facility enhance the security of the documents or records
